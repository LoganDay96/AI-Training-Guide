Meeting Cost Calculator
üîß Utilities Beginner ‚è± 5 min
Calculate the true cost of meetings based on attendee salaries and duration.
You'll build: Attendee input, duration slider, cost calculation, "is this meeting worth it?" insight
Create a Streamlit Meeting Cost Calculator with these features: INPUTS: - Number of attendees by role (dropdown with typical Walmart roles) - Meeting duration (slider: 15 min to 2 hours) - Meeting frequency (one-time, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly) - Optional: custom hourly rates CALCULATIONS: - Estimated hourly cost per role (use reasonable estimates) - Total meeting cost = (hours) x (sum of attendee hourly rates) - Annual cost for recurring meetings OUTPUT: 1. Big number: "This meeting costs $X" 2. If recurring: "Annual cost: $X,XXX" 3. Fun comparisons: "That's equivalent to X laptops" or "X hours of analyst time" 4. Tips: "Could this be an email?" prompt if many attendees + short duration 5. Breakdown: Pie chart of cost by role Make it fun and slightly cheeky while being genuinely useful!
