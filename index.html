What is Vibe Coding?

Using natural language (English) to tell an AI what you want - and the AI writes the code. You're just vibing while it works.

Realistic Expectations 0-60: Fast. Dashboards, analysis, visualizations, personal tools.
60-100: Slow. Production apps, live data, hosting - takes more effort.
